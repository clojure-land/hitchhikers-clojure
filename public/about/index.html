About

What is this?

This mix of a Clojure guide and fanfiction is a curious result of a marathon viewing session of Hitchhikers’s Guide to the Galaxy”, Gigasquid’s love of Clojure, and a questionable amount of cheese.

The source is on github. So if you want to help out by fixing spelling errors, grammar, or have great post ideas- pull requests are welcome.
